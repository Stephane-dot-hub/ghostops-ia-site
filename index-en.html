How does a GhostOps activation work?

The dispositif is designed to be deployable quickly by a CEO, a board or an HR leadership team, without building a consulting “factory”. Each activation follows a clear sequence, formalised in writing.

  1. Confidential brief via the contact page: perimeter concerned, current situation, key actors, critical deadlines.
  2. Reading & reply within 24 business hours: assessment of whether a GhostOps intervention is relevant, initial risk mapping, potential options (short crisis, governance, executive trajectory, etc.).
  3. Framed intervention sequence (30, 60 or 90 days, or trajectory support): perimeter, schedule, deliverables and fee framework formalised and validated before any deployment.

If the situation does not fall within the GhostOps perimeter, this is stated explicitly, without trying to “hold on” to the dossier. The objective is to focus our interventions on configurations where our value added is real and assumable.

About GhostOps

GhostOps is an independent tactical HR & narrative intervention cell, positioned at the interface of human resources, governance and operational artificial intelligence. We intervene for companies, boards and exposed executives when traditional consulting patterns are no longer sufficient and the issue is no longer to “think”, but to produce a controlled outcome.

Our operations cover HR crises, internal conflicts, decision bottlenecks, executive repositioning, management of invisible alliances, sensitive transitions, as well as narrative dominance in unstable environments. Generative AI is not a gadget: it is embedded in our playbooks to listen, simulate, test and continuously adjust action scenarios.

The GhostOps method relies on three principles: a rapid footprint (48 hours) to map tensions and key actors, short execution loops to align decisions with reality, and minimal exposure for our clients, with calibrated documentation (opposable when needed, discreet by default).

We always operate within a legal framework, with a constant concern for clean governance, compliance (including GDPR) and long-term reputation. GhostOps does not replace decision-making bodies: we equip them to regain control, discreetly, in contexts where a mis-reading can cost a mandate, a deal or a career trajectory.
