How does a GhostOps activation work?

The framework is designed to be activated quickly by a CEO, board or HR leadership without deploying a consulting “factory”. Each activation follows a clear sequence, formalised in writing.

  1. Confidential brief via the contact page: scope, current situation, key actors, critical deadlines.
  2. Review & answer within 24 business hours: assessment of whether a GhostOps intervention is relevant, first risks identified, possible options (short crisis mode, governance, executive trajectory, etc.).
  3. Framed intervention sequence (30, 60 or 90 days, or trajectory support): scope, timeline, deliverables and fee framework formalised and validated before deployment.

If the situation does not fall within the GhostOps perimeter, this is stated explicitly, without attempting to “keep” the case. The objective is to focus our interventions on configurations where our added value is real and assumable.

About GhostOps

GhostOps is an independent tactical HR & narrative intervention cell, positioned at the intersection of human resources, governance and operational AI. We intervene for companies, boards and exposed executives when traditional advisory models are no longer sufficient and the point is no longer to “reflect”, but to deliver a controlled outcome.

Our operations cover HR crises, internal conflicts, decision bottlenecks, executive repositioning, management of invisible alliances, sensitive transitions, and narrative dominance in unstable environments. Generative AI is not a gadget: it is embedded in our playbooks to listen, simulate, test and continuously adjust action scenarios.

The GhostOps method relies on three principles: a rapid footprint (48 hours) to map tensions and key actors, short execution loops to align decisions with reality, and minimal exposure for our clients, with calibrated documentation (usable if necessary, discreet by default).

We always operate within a legal framework, with a constant concern for cleaner governance, compliance (including GDPR) and long-term image protection. GhostOps does not replace decision-making bodies: we arm them to regain control, discreetly, in contexts where a poor reading can cost a mandate, a deal or a career trajectory.
